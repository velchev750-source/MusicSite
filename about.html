About Arte+

Founded through a shared passion for chamber music and artistic excellence, Arte+ brings together three accomplished musicians united by refined musical sensitivity and expressive collaboration. Blending piano, flute, and violin, the trio creates a distinctive sound that balances lyrical elegance with vibrant energy. Their repertoire spans classical masterworks, romantic favorites, and carefully curated selections tailored to private and formal occasions. Arte+ has performed at distinguished cultural events, private celebrations, and exclusive gatherings, earning recognition for their polished stage presence and nuanced interpretations. Known for their professionalism, adaptability, and attention to detail, the trio is valued not only for technical excellence but for their ability to shape a sophisticated and memorable musical atmosphere for every audience they encounter.

Anna

Anna is a refined and expressive violinist whose performances are marked by warmth, precision, and emotional depth. Trained in the classical tradition, she developed her artistry through years of dedicated study and chamber music collaboration. Her playing combines technical elegance with a natural sensitivity that allows each phrase to breathe and resonate. As a founding member of Arte+, Anna brings lyrical nuance and vibrant energy to the ensemble’s sound. Whether performing in intimate private settings or on distinguished concert stages, she approaches every appearance with grace, passion, and a deep respect for the music she interprets..

Arila

Arila is a graceful and accomplished pianist whose playing is distinguished by clarity, depth, and refined musical sensitivity. With a strong foundation in classical repertoire and a passion for chamber collaboration, she brings both technical precision and expressive warmth to every performance. As the pianist of Arte+, Arila shapes the ensemble’s sound with elegance and balance, creating a rich harmonic foundation that allows the music to unfold naturally. Whether performing at private celebrations or formal concert events, she approaches each occasion with artistry, poise, and a deep commitment to musical excellence.

Elena

Elena is a luminous and expressive flutist whose playing is characterized by clarity, elegance, and refined musical phrasing. With a strong background in classical performance and chamber music, she brings both technical agility and lyrical sensitivity to every interpretation. As a member of Arte+, Elena adds brilliance and color to the ensemble’s sound, shaping melodies with grace and fluidity. Whether performing in intimate gatherings or distinguished event settings, she creates an atmosphere of sophistication and warmth, connecting naturally with audiences through her poised and expressive artistry.
